Welcome to our site! Minority Prince George's is a project created by 4 university of Maryland students who sought to create an application that centralized information about PG County minority-owned businesses.

This endeavor hopes to facilitate easier discovery of local small business owned by members of communities that are historically underrepresented as business owners. Our site allows you to search for a type of business, see the ratings for a given business, find out how many miles away a given business is, and see the contact information for a given business.
